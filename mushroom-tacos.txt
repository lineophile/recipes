Ingredients
    - oyster mushrooms
    - woodear mushrooms
    - red bell pepper
    - pablano peppers
    - onion
    - garlic
    - cilantro, green onion for garnish
    - paprika, coriander, chili powder, salt, pepper for seasoning

Instructions

    1. Cut everything up
    2. Saute onion and garlic
    3. Add peppers and sliced onions, cook for 15 min. Season with salt and pepper
    4. Add mushrooms
    5. Serve with fresh salsa or slaw
